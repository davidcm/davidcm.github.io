Me

Currently working as a teacher and videogame developer at FXAnimation Barcelona 3D School, where I tutorize all the students' videogame projects and program them using the Unity engine. Also trying to be productive with my scarce free time to progress in my own independent projects.

Previously worked at EVENTLab, directed by Mel Slater, where I helped implement and execute a VR experiment with which we were able to measure presence in a virtual environment. A paper resulted from that and was published at SIGGRAPH. We used a MoCap suit, an HMD and more.

Also worked at T-Systems CRV Barcelona, a team of experts in interactive VR projects for commercial purposes, using technologies such as CAVE, Powerwall and HMD. There I worked creating 3D assets, extending their propietary engine scripting features, etc.
