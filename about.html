Myself

The name is David Corominas Montes.

Currently employed as a teacher and videogame developer at FXAnimation Barcelona 3D School, where I tutorize all the students' videogame projects and program them using the Unity engine.

Previously worked at EVENTLab, led by Mel Slater, where I helped to implement and execute a VR experiment with which we were able to measure presence in a virtual environment. A paper resulted from that and was published at SIGGRAPH. We used an optical MoCap system, an HMD and more.

Also worked at T-Systems CRV Barcelona, a team of experts in interactive VR projects for commercial purposes, using technologies such as CAVE, Powerwall and AR. There I worked creating 3D assets, extending their engine scripting features, etc.
